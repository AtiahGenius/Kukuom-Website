Who We Are

The Kukuom Cooperative Cocoa Farmers and Marketing Union is a farmer-led organization supporting cocoa farmers through training, sustainability programs, and fair market access to improve incomes and strengthen communities.

Mission

To improve the living conditions of our members through fair and sustainable cocoa production.

Vision

To ensure cocoa farming families achieve sustainable livelihoods and reach their full potential.

  • Farmer-owned and democratically governed cooperative
  • Established to improve livelihoods through cocoa farming
  • Members organized into societies across farming communities
  • Committed to transparency, accountability, and participation
