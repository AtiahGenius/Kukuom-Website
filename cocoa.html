Cocoa Farming in Kukuom, Ghana

Cocoa farming is the backbone of the Kukuom community. For generations, families have cultivated cocoa as their primary source of income, supporting education, healthcare, and local development.

The region’s fertile soil and favorable climate make it ideal for producing high-quality cocoa beans that meet both local and international standards.
