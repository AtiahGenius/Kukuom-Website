About Us

Building sustainable livelihoods through fair and responsible cocoa production.

Who We Are...

The Kukuom Cooperative Cocoa Farmers and Marketing Union is a farmer-led organization based in Kukuom in Ghana’s Ahafo Region. The cooperative unites 3,782 cocoa farmers across 29 communities to promote sustainable agriculture, fair trade, and improved livelihoods.

The Kukuom Cooperative Cocoa Farmers and Marketing Union is a registered cooperative made up of cocoa farmers from the South Asunafo District in the Ahafo Region of Ghana. The cooperative has its main office in the town of Kukuom and serves farming communities spread across the district.

Since achieving Fairtrade certification in 2012, we have strengthened farmer incomes, empowered women, and invested in community development projects that improve health, education, and access to clean water.

Our Mission

To improve the living conditions of our members through fair and sustainable cocoa production by providing them with the means to secure a decent income.

Our Vision

To ensure cocoa farming families achieve sustainable livelihoods and realize their full potential.
