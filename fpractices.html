Farming Practices

Sustainable methods that ensure high-quality cocoa and protect our environment.

Traditional Knowledge Meets Sustainable Innovation

Cocoa farming in Kukuom combines generations of indigenous knowledge with modern sustainable techniques. Farmers carefully manage soil health, shade trees, and crop diversity to maintain long-term productivity.

Our Sustainable Farming Methods
  • Agroforestry: Growing cocoa under shade trees to preserve biodiversity
  • Organic soil enrichment using compost and natural fertilizers
  • Water conservation through mulching and ground cover
  • Integrated pest management to reduce chemical use
