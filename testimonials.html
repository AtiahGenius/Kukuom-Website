Stories from our farmers - real voices, real impact

Before receiving this support, life was extremely difficult as a widow raising my children alone. Providing daily meals and paying school fees was a constant struggle. When I received this amount, it felt like a turning point in my life. I have now invested in a plantain retail business.

Boakye Asomani

A New Beginning Through Support

“

At first, I doubted whether this promise would truly become a reality. Receiving the support changed my perspective completely. I am now expanding my rice farm in a way that protects the environment while increasing my income. This opportunity has given me confidence.

Asamoah Nyamekye

From Uncertainty to Sustainable Farming

“

For many years, I depended on high-interest loans just to keep my business running. Since joining the savings group, everything has changed. I can now save consistently and access affordable loans when needed. This has brought me true financial independence.

Ama Nsiah

Financial Freedom Through Savings

“

Saving money used to feel impossible because there was always something urgent to spend on. After joining the group and learning how to manage my finances, I began to see real change. Within two years, I have started building my own three-bedroom house in my village. This is something I never thought I could achieve. Now, I feel more secure, empowered, and motivated to keep improving my future.

Mosa Martha

Turning Dreams Into Reality

“

Through the leadership training, I gained the confidence and skills to step forward and lead. Today, I serve as a union president, advocating for women and supporting others in my community. This journey has transformed how I see myself and what I believe is possible. I am now able to influence positive change and help other women find their voice and opportunities for growth.

Akua Nyarko

Leadership and Empowerment

“

Before joining the project, I didn’t know the exact size of my farm and often wasted resources. After my farm was mapped, I now plan better and use my resources efficiently. Joining the cooperative has also opened new opportunities for improving my productivity and income.

Nkrumah Yaw
