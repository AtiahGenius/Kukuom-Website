Processing & Quality Control

Careful post-harvest handling ensures premium cocoa quality.

From Pod to Premium Beans

After harvesting, cocoa beans undergo a series of carefully managed processes that develop flavor, ensure safety, and meet international quality standards.

Fermentation: Developing Flavor

Fermentation is a crucial stage that enhances the chocolate flavor. Beans are placed in covered boxes or heaps for several days, allowing natural microbial activity to break down sugars and develop aroma.

Sun Drying for Preservation

After fermentation, beans are sun-dried to reduce moisture content. Proper drying prevents mold growth and ensures beans can be safely stored and transported.
