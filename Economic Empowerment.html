Economic Empowerment

Strengthening livelihoods through sustainable cocoa production.

Improving Financial Stability

Cocoa farming provides a reliable source of income for families in Kukuom. Through improved farming practices, market access, and fair pricing, farmers are able to achieve greater financial security and plan for the future.

Key Economic Benefits
  • Increased and more stable household incomes
  • Job creation in farming, processing, and transportation
  • Support for small businesses and local markets
  • Financial independence for women and youth

Better Farming

Higher Yields Increased Income
Local Businesses Grow Stronger Economy
Empowering Women & Youth

Economic opportunities in cocoa farming enable women and young people to participate actively in the local economy. Training programs and access to resources help them build sustainable livelihoods and contribute to community development.

About

The Kukuom Cooperative Cocoa Farmers and Marketing Union is a farmer-led organization based in Kukuom in Ghana’s Ahafo Region.
